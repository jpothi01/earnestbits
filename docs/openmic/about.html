OpenMic App

OpenMic is an app for capturing performances at open mic events.

Download on the App Store

FAQs

What's this app for?

OpenMic has two main functions:

  • Record and organize video recordings of artistic performances
  • Alert performers when their alloted time is up

Typically, the event organizer sets up an iPhone on a tripod in front of the stage. This way, the performers can easily see the warning light.

How do I get the performance videos out of the app?

Click on a performance recording, then hit the share button at the top right. This will export as an mp4.

Can I sync the videos with cloud storage?

Not yet, but let me know if you'd like this functionality.

Privacy Policy

OpenMic does not collect any user data. All data is kept on-device.
